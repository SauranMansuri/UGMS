About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare of the Mansuri community in Uttar Gujarat.

Uttar Gujarat Mansuri Samaj (Gandhinagar) Trust is a beacon of hope and support for the Mansuri community in Uttar Gujarat. Established in 2010 with the vision of uplifting the community, we have been tirelessly working towards providing better opportunities and resources for our members.

Our trust is built on the principles of unity, compassion, and service. We believe in empowering individuals through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. Our dedicated team of volunteers and professionals work round the clock to ensure that our community members receive the best possible support and guidance.

We are proud of our heritage and strive to preserve our cultural values while embracing modern advancements. Our trust organizes various cultural events, workshops, and seminars to promote awareness and foster a sense of belonging among our community members.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services including educational support, healthcare, and community development programs.

  • Educational Support: To motivate students through prize distribution, zakat and tuition fee help.
  • Healthcare: Regular health camps, medical check-ups, and health awareness programs.
  • Community Development: Skill development programs, vocational training, and women empowerment initiatives.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and awareness programs.
  • Cultural Events: Organizing cultural events, workshops, and seminars to promote cultural heritage.
  • Emergency Relief: Providing aid and support during natural disasters and emergencies.
